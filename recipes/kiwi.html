Dehydrated Kiwi Instant Pot

Description

Dehydrated Kiwis Will Be Your New Grab-and-Go Snack

Ingredients

  • 2 kiwis
  • air fryer

Steps

  1. Peel the kiwi with a paring knife or a vegetable peeler.
  2. Slice the kiwi into ¼ inch or ½ inch slices. When you slice the kiwi thinner, it dehydrates faster. Keep this in mind if you want your kiwi done sooner.
  3. Cover the dehydrator and set the temperature to 135 degrees Fahrenheit.
  4. Check the kiwi after six hours. Depending on how thickly you sliced the kiwi, it could take up to 12 hours for them to be completely dehydrated.
  5. Once 5 minutes has elapsed, release any remaining pressure in Instant Pot by using a wooden spoon or kitchen towel to knock vent knob to vent position.
