Hard Boiled Eggs Instant Pot

Description

The perfect technique for cooking hard boiled eggs in the pressure cooker is the 5-5-5 method.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • half dozen eggs

Steps

  1. Place 1 cup of COLD water in the inner pot of pressure cooker (liquid is ALWAYS needed for the pressure cooker to be able to reach pressure.
  2. Place eggs in egg rack or carefully on top of the rack that the Instant Pot came with and seal.
  3. Set cook time to 5 minutes (5 minutes on high pressure--push the manual button and adjust the timer to 5 minutes)
  4. After cook time has elapsed, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes (I suggest setting a timer that will beep at 5 minutes so you don't lose track of time. )
  5. Once 5 minutes has elapsed, release any remaining pressure in Instant Pot by using a wooden spoon or kitchen towel to knock vent knob to vent position.
  6. Carefully, remove eggs from the pressure cooker and place it in an ice bath. Eggs are VERY hot, so use potholder if your fingers aren't made of steel!
  7. After eggs have been in an ice bath for 5 minutes, remove eggs from the ice bath.
